Detailed Description

This trait removes the const and volatile qualifications from the actual type, instead of the top-level reference or pointer category.

Following some examples comparing the behaviour of std::remove_cv with sl::remove_type_cv.

https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/types/remove_cv
