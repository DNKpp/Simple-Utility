Detailed Description

This trait adds the const and volatile qualifications to the actual type, instead of the top-level reference or pointer category.

Following some examples comparing the behaviour of std::add_cv with sl::add_type_cv.

See also
https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/types/add_cv
STATIC_REQUIRE(std::same_as<int&, std::add_cv_t<int&>>);
STATIC_REQUIRE(std::same_as<const volatile int&, sl::add_type_cv_t<int&>>);
